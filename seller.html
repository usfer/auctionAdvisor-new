Advantages of Auction Sale

PREDEFINED SALE AND CLOSING DATES

Plans can be made for the future. Timing is a crucial element in today’s marketplace.

SELLER DEFINED TERMS & CONDITIONS

The Seller controls the sale process, and is not subjected to buyer imposed conditions.

PRE-QUALIFIED BUYERS: NON-REFUNDABLE CASH DEPOSIT

The successful bidder has a financial incentive to close.

“AS-IS” “NON-CONTINGENT” BUYER PAYS FEES & CLOSING COSTS

The sale is not subject to inspection results, financial contingencies or other issues, which cause traditional sales to fall out of escrow.

COMPETITIVE BIDDING ENVIRONMENT

There is no limit as to how much the properties may sell for and pricing is ultimately determined by buyer emotion.

DYNAMIC MARKETING EXPOSURE

The properties will benefit from exposure, not typical to traditional real estate sales, as all promotional efforts focus around a specific sale date and create a sense of urgency.

Auction Strategies

Absolute Auction

  • No reserve or minimum price
  • The property is sold to the highest bidder, regardless of the price
  • Buyer excitement and participation are heightened since a sale is guaranteed
  • Generates maximum response from the market place and attracts the highest amount of interest

Subject to a Minimum Bid, Without Published Reserve

  • A minimum bid is not published, and the seller reserves the right to accept or reject the highest bid within a specified time
  • Seller predetermines the price at which the property will be sold and is not obligated to confirm a sale other than at a price that is entirely acceptable to them
  • Prospective buyers may not invest the time and expense of due diligence when there is no certainty they will be able to buy the property even if they are the highest bidder

Minimum Bid, With Reserve

  • The lowest price the seller is willing to accept is stated in the brochure, advertisements and is announced at the auction
  • Buyers know they will be able to buy at or above the minimum bid
  • Once that bid is reached, the property will sell to the highest bidder
  • Reduced risk for the seller as the sales price must be above a minimum acceptable level
